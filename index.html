Why we import cars from the United States?

We compared the US markets with the European ones and realized that buying a car in America is several times more profitable, no matter how paradoxical it may sound. This is due to well-thought-out logistics, the level of development of services for assessing the condition of a car and the process of buying a car itself.

Most US citizens lease a car for several years, and the leasing company itself provides ongoing maintenance for the car during the entire period of operation, which is why cars from the US are one of the best choices for car enthusiasts.

Why such a low price?

Damaged cars from the USA are bought at auctions of insurance companies. At these auctions, the car loses half its price even due to minimal damage. If you take into account the monetary costs, namely the buyout, delivery, customs and repair, then the price of a similar car in the same condition will be higher by 35-50%, and new ones will cost astronomical money.
